PRODUCT INTRODUCTION

Clinized 5mg tablet is a medicine used to treat high blood pressure (hypertension). It belongs to a class of medicines known as calcium channel blockers, which help to lower blood pressure. This helps prevent heart attacks and strokes.

Clinized 5mg tablet may be prescribed alone or along with other medicines. The dose depends on severity of your condition. You can take it any time of day with or without food, but it is the best to take it at the same time each day. Keep taking it for long as adviced by your doctor. Do not stop this medicine by your own, even if you feel well, because high blood pressure has often no symptoms. This makes it hard to identify any sudden spike and if you stop taking it your condition may get worse.
